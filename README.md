# 🛍️ Customer Segmentation & Retention Analysis  
*Transforming customer data into strategic insights for business growth.*

---

## 💡 **Project Overview**
This project explores how customer analytics and process redesign can strengthen retention and drive revenue for an e-commerce business.  
I worked with a real dataset from Kaggle to understand customer behavior, segment buyers using RFM analysis, and design a solution framework that combines data-driven decisions with business strategy.  

Through this project, I aimed to bring together all the skills I developed through my **Microsoft Business Analyst specialization** — from requirement gathering and stakeholder mapping to process modeling and dashboard storytelling.

---

## 🎯 **Purpose**
The goal of this project was not just to analyze numbers, but to **think like a Business Analyst** — understanding the *why* behind customer patterns and *how* the business could act on them.  
This project simulates a real consulting scenario where data meets business problem-solving — much like how firms such as **Bain & Company** approach client challenges.

## 🌐 **Data Source**
Dataset: [E-Commerce Data (Kaggle)](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/carrie1/ecommerce-data)

## ⚙️ **Project Features & Highlights**

### 🧩 **Business Problem**
The company was facing **low customer retention** and lacked a structured engagement strategy.  
With 78% of customers being one-time buyers, the challenge was to identify profitable customer segments and design strategies to retain them.

---

### 🎯 **Goal of the Dashboard**
To create a clear, actionable dashboard that helps marketing and sales teams:  
- Identify **high-value** and **at-risk** customers.  
- Track customer purchase frequency and spending patterns.  
- Visualize loyalty campaign outcomes and KPIs.  
- Support decision-making through data-driven insights.

---

### 🧭 **Project Walkthrough**
1. **Business Understanding & Stakeholder Mapping** – Defined business goals and created a RACI matrix to clarify ownership.  
2. **Process Mapping (As-Is & To-Be)** – Mapped current customer journey, identified gaps, and redesigned the To-Be process for stronger engagement.  
3. **Data Preparation** – Cleaned and structured data in Excel; removed cancellations, duplicates, and missing CustomerIDs.  
4. **Customer Segmentation (RFM Model)** – Segmented customers into categories like “Loyal,” “At-Risk,” and “New” based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary scores.  
5. **Dashboard Development (Power BI)** – Built an interactive dashboard to visualize segments, purchase trends, and KPIs.  
6. **Solution Design (Visio Swimlane Diagram)** – Created an actionable plan showing the roles of Marketing, Sales, Support, and Analyst teams.  
7. **Business Impact Analysis** – Quantified opportunities and proposed recommendations to increase repeat purchases.

### 💼 **Business Impact & Insights**
- Identified that **78% of buyers were one-time customers**, highlighting a major retention gap.  
- Proposed **personalized campaigns and follow-up processes** projected to improve repeat purchase rates by **20%**.  
- Discovered that **20% of customers contribute to over 60% of total revenue**, reinforcing the need for loyalty strategies.  
- Designed a **data-driven roadmap** that connects marketing actions to measurable KPIs and revenue impact.  
- Demonstrated how structured business analysis can bridge data analytics with strategy formulation.
