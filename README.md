📌 Business Problem: Low customer retention and lack of targeted engagement strategy
🛍️ Customer Segmentation & Retention Analysis

Transforming customer data into actionable business insights using RFM analysis.

💡 Project Overview:

This project analyzes customer transaction data from an e-commerce business to identify key customer segments and improve retention strategies.

Using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis, customers were segmented into actionable groups such as High Value, Loyal, and At-Risk, enabling targeted business decisions.

🎯 Objective:
Identify high-value and at-risk customers
Understand purchasing behavior
Enable data-driven retention and marketing strategies

🧰 Tools & Technologies:
Microsoft Excel → Data cleaning, pivot tables, RFM segmentation
Microsoft Visio → Process mapping (As-Is & To-Be)
Power BI Service → Dashboard visualization
Canva → Business presentation
Microsoft Word → Documentation & business case

🌐 Dataset:
Source: Kaggle (E-commerce dataset)
Contains transaction-level data including CustomerID, InvoiceDate, Quantity, and Revenue

⚙️ Approach:
Data Cleaning
* Removed cancellations, duplicates, missing CustomerIDs
Aggregation (Excel Pivot Tables)
* Last Purchase Date (Recency)
* Number of Purchases (Frequency)
* Total Spend (Monetary)
RFM Segmentation
* Customers scored on R, F, M (1–5 scale)
* Percentile-based scoring used for Frequency & Monetary
Customer Segmentation
* High Value
* Loyal
* At Risk
* Regular
Dashboard & Visualization
* Built interactive dashboard to track customer behavior and revenue contribution

📊 Key Insights:

~20% of customers contribute to a majority of revenue
A significant portion of customers fall into the At-Risk segment
High-frequency customers show strong loyalty and upsell potential
Customer retention presents a major growth opportunity

💼 Business Recommendations:
🎯 Retain High Value customers through loyalty programs
⚠️ Re-engage At-Risk customers with targeted campaigns
🔁 Upsell Loyal customers with premium offerings
📈 Nurture Regular customers into higher-value segments

Translated data insights into business strategies
